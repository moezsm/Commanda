# Commanda

> **Mobile-first order management for social media sellers.**

Commanda is a simple, mobile-first SaaS application that helps social media sellers manage orders, track deliveries, and organize customers — all in one place. Built specifically for Facebook and Instagram sellers.

## Project Summary

Commanda is planned as a freemium SaaS for small and micro sellers who receive orders through social media and need a simple way to record and follow each order from confirmation to delivery.

The MVP focuses on one critical workflow: after receiving an order, the seller opens Commanda on mobile and saves the order with the product, client information, delivery company, and delivery details. Each seller has a fully separate workspace for orders, clients, and delivery records.

The launch market is Tunisia, where affordability, mobile usage, and operational simplicity are essential. The long-term expansion path is France and selected African markets, which means the product should be designed from the start for localization, multi-courier workflows, and country-specific premium modules. Premium features will be introduced gradually, with e-invoicing intentionally postponed to a later stage.

## 🎯 Who Is Commanda For?

Commanda is designed for **small sellers who run their business on social media**, particularly on Facebook and Instagram. If you recognize yourself in any of the following, Commanda was built for you:

- You sell products directly through Facebook posts, groups, or Marketplace.
- You take orders through Instagram DMs or story polls.
- You track orders in notebooks, spreadsheets, or scattered chat threads.
- You juggle multiple buyers at once and sometimes lose track of who ordered what.
- You spend more time managing logistics than actually selling.
- You can't afford expensive enterprise software and need something lightweight that works on your phone.

## ❓ Why Commanda? — The Problem We Solve

Running a small business on social media is exciting, but managing it is chaotic. Social platforms were never designed for commerce, which means sellers face real, daily pain points:

| Problem | How Commanda Helps |
|---|---|
| Orders arrive scattered across DMs, comments, and messages | Centralized order inbox — one place for every order |
| No easy way to track which orders have been shipped or delivered | Built-in delivery tracking so nothing falls through the cracks |
| Customer details (address, phone, purchase history) are buried in chats | Customer profiles that keep all information organized and searchable |
| Mistakes happen when managing everything manually | Simple workflows reduce human error and save time |
| No affordable tool built for the mobile seller | Fully mobile-first — designed to be used from your phone, on the go |

Commanda gives the solo seller or micro-business owner the operational backbone they need — without the complexity or cost of enterprise tools.
